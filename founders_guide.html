The Founder's Guide to AI Solutions

This guide is to help you be clear with different types of AI Solutions.


The reason I'm sharing this guide is because, I have been there. It was very confusing to understand even after coming from a technical background.


And I know that not all founders can afford to spend time on understanding such complex and overwhelming terms as there are a lot of things to focus on.


So here you go.

Business Purpose & Value Differentiation

🔄

Workflow Automation

Purpose:

Improve efficiency, reduce manual effort, ensure consistency for repetitive processes

Business Value:

Cost savings, speed, error reduction in operational tasks

Best For:

Connecting disparate systems, automating predefined sequences

🤖

Single Complex Agent

Purpose:

Provide intelligent interface performing sophisticated, coherent tasks

Business Value:

New AI-driven capabilities, enhanced user interaction, unified AI functions

Best For:

Complex tasks requiring unified intelligence (like "Jarvis")

🌐

Multi-Agent System (MAS)

Purpose:

Solve complex problems through distributed intelligence and emergent collaboration

Business Value:

Robust adaptive solutions, scalable problem-solving, handling complex coordination

Best For:

Dynamic environments requiring decentralized decision-making
